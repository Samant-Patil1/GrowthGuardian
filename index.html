Educational AI and Agriculture

One stone, two birds: Advancing education and agriculture through technology.

Overview

Educational AI and Robot: This system collects health parameters of students, creates reports, detects power consumption of smart boards, and aims to improve education.

Agricultural AI: This system helps farmers manage farms better, predicts weather, monitors farm data, and improves crop health with AI-equipped machinery.
